Kobe Japan Steakhouse, Seafood, Sushi Lounge and Sports Bar

Join the Birthday Club and get a $30 certificate to spend on Hibachi dining Monday through Thursday nights during the month of your birthday! Our entertaining chefs cook right at your table and will help make your birthday special (see terms and faqs).

At Kobe Japan Restaurant where you'll not only find the very best Japanese cuisine this side of the Bay, but also the most entertaining chefs as they prepare your Teppanyaki meal before your very eyes. Kobe Japan prides itself in serving the finest Kobe beef from Snake River Farm. Let us entertain you at one of our seven Teppanyaki tables where our talented chefs will prepare your meal of Kobe Beef, Maine Lobster Tail, or one of our Hibachi Classic or Hibachi Combination plates.

Open seven days a week for lunch and dinner per the schedule below

Holiday Hours — We are open on Christmas Eve and New Year's Eve for lunch from 11:30am to 2:30pm and dinner from 5-10pm with Happy Hour from 4–6pm; Christmas Day and New Year's Day we're open 12-9pm for lunch and dinner.

Kobe also caters to those who wish to choose from an eclectic selection of sushi, sashimi, or traditional Bento Boxes. We have weekly sushi board specials that change every Thursday and a huge selection of sushi rolls with unrivaled freshness and taste. Come join us for our own Kobe Roll or special rolls from sushi chefs Tom and Richard. We also serve a wide variety of cold sake from the sweet tasting Nigori to the very dry Junmai, over 20 varieties to choose from along with our traditional house sake served warm.

Have a Birthday comming up or a sports enthusiasts?

For the sports enthusiasts, Kobe Japan boasts a full bar and lounge featuring six high definition flat screen TVs for all your sports entertainment. Our bar offers eight beer varieties on tap, including Budweiser and Sapporo along with several unique bottled beer and ale brands. All of your favorite mixed drink cocktails are available as well, along with several varietals of wine. Be sure to check-out our Sports Bar Happy Hour Specials.

Have a Birthday or Anniversary coming up? We cater to special occasions with private dining available in either our Teppenyaki or Sushi Banquet Rooms. We're here to serve all your special celebration and group party needs.
